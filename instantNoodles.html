How to make Instant Noodles

    Ingredients

  • Cup of instant noodles

  • Either a microwave or a kettle

  • Water

    Steps!!teehee

  1. Open up cup of instant noodles

      2. Option 1

    1. Boil water using kettle

    2. Pour the water in the cup heheheheheheh

    3. Wait for 3-5 min

    4. Take off the lid and you're done :)

      Option 2

    1. Pour water in the cup

    2. Reseal Cup

    3. Put in the microwave for roughly 5 mins

Done!
